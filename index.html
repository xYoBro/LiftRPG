LiftRPG

A workout journal fused with a branching narrative TTRPG. Real training. One encrypted ending. Written in pencil.

How it Works
  1. Generate a Prompt — enter your workout (Liftosaur or plain text) and a story idea
  2. Paste it into any LLM — Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini — and save the JSON it returns
  3. Upload the JSON to Render — print your saddle-stitched booklet
  4. Train the program for six weeks, pencil in hand
  5. Return to Render with your assembled password to unlock the ending
