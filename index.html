A workout journal fused with a branching narrative TTRPG. Real training. One encrypted ending. Written in pencil.

How it Works
  1. Start a Booklet — enter your workout (Liftosaur or plain text) and your booklet direction
  2. Choose a guided build or run the full booklet build here with your own API key
  3. Upload the JSON to Renderer — check pages and print your saddle-stitched booklet
  4. Train the program for six weeks, pencil in hand
  5. Return to Renderer, load your booklet JSON, then enter the password to unlock the ending
